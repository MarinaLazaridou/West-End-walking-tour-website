West End Walking Tour

The West End is an area in central London and is famous for its old and remarkable theatres. It can be characterised as an area that combines modern and historic architecture with entertainment. All visitors have a unique opportunity to explore some of the city's most popular attractions and museums, dine and shop.

The following map depicts information about an organised walking tour within the West End area. The tour includes the viewing of famous theatres, parks and squares, and introduces the visitors to the exploration of one of the most beautiful, artistic and lively areas of London.

The tour's starting point is the Phoenix Theatre. The final place of the tour is Covent Garden, where the visitors have the opportunity to rest and enjoy incredible views while exploring this remarkable area of the city.
